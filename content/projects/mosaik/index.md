MOSAIK enables cross-platform integration of spatial transcriptomics data from Xenium (10x Genomics), CosMx (NanoString), and MERFISH (Vizgen) into a common SpatialData framework.

The toolkit standardises coordinate systems, gene panels, and metadata to enable fair comparison and joint analysis across platforms.
